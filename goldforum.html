Who We Are

The Manufacturer
Behind Your Showcase

Gold Forum is a wholesale jewelry manufacturer based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. We work directly with jewelleries, retailers, and boutique stores — supplying high-quality gold and silver ornaments that stock your shelves and define your brand.

We are not a retail store. Our focus is entirely on supporting businesses that sell jewelry — giving them the supply reliability, branding capabilities, and customization expertise they need to stand out in a competitive market.

Whether you need a consistent wholesale supply, want to stamp your identity onto every ornament you sell, or have customers asking for one-of-a-kind pieces — Gold Forum delivers.

What sets Gold Forum apart
  • Direct manufacturer — no middlemen, better pricing for retailers
  • Expert in both gold and silver ornament manufacturing
  • Branding & hallmarking on your ornaments to build retail identity
  • Custom design capability for end customers' unique requirements
  • Flexible order sizes — from bulk wholesale to individual custom pieces
  • Coimbatore-based with deep roots in the Tamil Nadu jewelry trade
